Top Places To Visit in The Philippines

Vigan City

Located in the Ilocos Norte Region, it's famous for preserving the look of the architecture during the Spanish Period. Having cobblestone streets, horse-drawn carriages (kalesa), and old mansions. It is visited to enjoy its scenery and feel like being travelled to the Spanish Era at again.

Boracay

An island known for its famous white sand, crystal clear water, this beach is visited by millions of tourists every year. Located in Malay, Aklan, it sure is a great place for people who are always ready to bask in the sun and getaway from the city life to have a tropical escape and fun.

Binondo

Here in the City of Manila, Binondo is considered as the oldest chinatown in the world. Selling Chinese goods to buy but however, ain't the only thing to do. Its also a great place to have a foodtrip which a lot of the locals here do. Planning a foodtrip? This is the place for you.

Underground River

In Puerto Princesa, Palawan, it's considered as a Subterranean River National Park and pretty limestone karst but also having a river flowing underground and showcases biodiversity with its fascinating 'mountain to sea' ecosystem, a UNESCO World Heritage.

Hundred Islands

A place in Northern side, this Island in Alaminos, Pangasinan is great for island hopping, snorkelling, and more, which is visited by lots of people yearly. However, do not be fooled by the word "Hundred", for there are actually 124 islands, but 123 at high-tide islands.
